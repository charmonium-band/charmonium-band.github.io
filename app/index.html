Charmonium the band

About

Charmonium is a mixture of Space and Krautrock with some blues motives inspired by Kraftwerk, Föllakzoid, Guru Freakout!, Space Debris etc.

Prepare to experience trips to space and back to the sounds of space missions of the past, analog synths and hypnotic bass guitar grooves...

***

This project is a remote collaboration of two friends that happen to live in different countries:

  • - Peter Matthies: keys, synths, guitar and vocals
  • - Dmitriy Borodiy (electric & bass guitar, drumpad)
Previously, we both used to play in a (now non-existent) band "The Cookies".

This is as indie a band as you can get, since we're both enthusiasts and make music mostly for our own pleasure whenever we have free time and energy.
