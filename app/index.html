BIO

This project is a remote collaboration of two friends that happen to live in different countries: Peter Mathias (mostly keys, synths, guitar and vocals) and Dmitriy Borodiy (electric & bass guitar, drumpad). Previously, we both used to play in a (now non-existent) band "The Cookies".

This is as indie a band as you can get, since we're both enthusiasts and make music mostly for our own pleasure whenever we have free time and energy. Being independent from anyone, we're therefore free to explore any musical dimensions we want.
